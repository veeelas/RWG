About Us

RWG is an innovative game development studio based in the picturesque city of Rivne, located in western Ukraine. Founded by a team of game dev enthusiasts and creative artists in 2018, RWG has already managed to impress the world with its unique vision and high-quality games.

Our mission is to make you happy with the best games on the Unity platform. Our team consists of experienced programmers, talented graphic artists, animators, and designers who work together to create immersive virtual worlds.

One of our biggest successes is the game "Galactic Odyssey," which has been recognized by players around the world for its incredible graphics, immersive gameplay, and engaging story. We are also known for our state-of-the-art approach to virtual reality and multiplayer gaming, allowing players to communicate and compete with each other in our games.

The RWG team is always open to cooperation and new ideas. We believe that games are not just entertainment, but also a way to unite people, teach them new things, and inspire them to achieve great goals.

Come visit us in Rivne, and we will be happy to show you our latest developments and share our emotions from creating the best games on Unity. RWG is your partner in the world of virtual adventures and unforgettable games!
