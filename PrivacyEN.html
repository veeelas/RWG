RWG Video Game Development Company Privacy Policy

1. General provisions

This Privacy Policy describes how RWG collects, uses, discloses, and protects personal information from our customers, partners, employees, and other stakeholders in connection with the development of video games.

2. Gathering information

2.1 Personal information

RWG may collect personal information, which includes, but is not limited to, the following data:

● Name, surname, contact information (e-mail, phone number).

● Information about payment details and financial data, if necessary, to pay for services or goods.

● Information you provide to us in connection with cooperation, video game development projects, technical specifications and requirements.

2.2 Automatically collected information

We may automatically collect certain information when you visit our website or use our services, including IP addresses, device types, browsers, time of visit, and the pages you view.

3. Use of information

RWG uses the information collected in order to:

● Provide game developer services, including video game development and support.

● Ensure accounting and payment processing, if necessary.

● Ensuring communication with customers, resolving their requests and suggestions.

● Improving the quality of our services and market analysis.

● Fulfillment of legal obligations and regulatory requirements.

4. Disclosure of information

RWG will not disclose your personal information to third parties without your consent, except as necessary to provide services or comply with legal obligations. We may disclose information:

● Partners and contractors who work with us to provide services.

● In cases where it is necessary in accordance with the law or at the request of law enforcement agencies.

5. Protection of information

We take the necessary measures to protect information from unauthorized access, loss, alteration or disclosure. We use technical, physical and administrative data protection measures.

6. Your rights

You have the right:

● Request access to the personal information we hold about you.

● To change or delete your personal data, if it is possible within the law.

● Withdraw your consent to data processing, if you have previously provided it.

7. Changes to the privacy policy

RWG may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Please check this document periodically to review the latest changes.
